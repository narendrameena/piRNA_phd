# Fig_SV_TE

**TE biology of SVs at piRNA loci**

- **Shows:** SVs rearrange loci; 87.6% overlap TEs (LINE/L1 + SINE dominate); disrupted loci carry 2× SVs; SV burden ∝ divergence.
- **How:** Fig_SV_TE.py. RepeatMasker C57BL_6NJ BED ∩ SVs (DEL=region, INS=±500 bp).
- **Data:** all_strains_SV_matrix.csv + RM BED
- **Provenance:** 2026-05-21 matrices.

Full raw→figure pipeline: [`PIPELINE.md`](PIPELINE.md). Originals under `analysis/claude_biomni_analysis/`.
